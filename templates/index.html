INSIGEN

A new approach for topic modelling

In Natural Language Processing (NLP), extracting valuable insights from extensive textual data is a persistent challenge. Top2Vec, a notable algorithm in topic modeling, is recognized for uncovering topics without predefined labels. Insigen builds on this by offering a generalized topic distribution for documents, determining associations, and generating informative extractive summaries. Results suggest Insigen as a promising solution for document analysis, advancing topic modeling, keyword extraction, and summarization beyond Top2Vec capabilities.
